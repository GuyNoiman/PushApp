# Version Roadmap — 4 Versions Ahead

Status: **Drafted 2026-07-08** (with founder). Living document — stage/version assignments evolve.

> Ranks **all remaining work** into four sequential versions. Maps onto the staging framework
> in `Product_Roadmap_and_Scope.md`: **V1 = POC · V2 = MVP · V3 = Commercial · V4 = Scale/Ecosystem.**
> POC/MVP detail lives in `POC_and_MVP_Scope.md` (D13/D14). The governing rule holds: **never
> shrink the vision — move a feature to a later version, don't delete it.**

---

## V1 — POC · *"Does social + Buddy drive persistence?"*

Goal: validate the core bet with the thinnest build. Success = users persist ~4 weeks and
complete/progress a Journey, crediting friends and/or Buddy.

- Journey **create** (name · rhythm · a few Steps · **Starter Step** · the **"why"**)
- **Check-in loop** + Home "what to do now"
- **Buddy** — evolves, reacts to check-ins, celebrates completions
- **Social** — add friends → **Allies** see progress → **cheer / nudge**; see theirs too
- **Coin economy + Shop** — earn coins → Buddy cosmetics (shallow: coins only, small set)
- **Missions + Login rewards** (shallow) — the return loop
- **Basic reminders** (time / day; no AI)

---

## V2 — MVP · *"A product you'd adopt solo and keep for months"*

Goal: POC made adoptable and lightly personalized; stands alone without AI (D2).

- **Explore + a starter Journey library** — browse & **adopt** existing Journeys (fixes cold-start)
- **Proper onboarding** — real first-run incl. **egg → hatch**
- **Journey Phases + full Journey types** (frequency · completion · avoidance · critical-compliance · hybrid)
- **Light AI (enhancement only):** personalized **encouragement** from the "why" + **smarter-timed reminders**
- Polish: **Journey-completion celebrations**, empty/return-to-life states, quick check-in variants
- **Grace Tokens (minimal)** — earned grace to skip/postpone a Step without breaking the Journey, with a brief reason capture (directly reduces the miss→quit churn). See Bible §36.

---

## V3 — Commercial · *"First polished, paid version"*

Goal: something people pay for; deepen the loops and turn on monetization.

- **Adaptive Intervention Engine** — multi-signal (time · location · missed-streak recovery), beyond smarter reminders
- **Weekly-planning flow** (§34.7)
- **AI Buddy-drafts-your-Journey** (paid tier) + conversational refinement
- **Achievements** wall (medals, conditions, detail sheet)
- **Deeper economy** — a second currency (e.g. gems), richer Shop, offers / "purchases-left"
- **Buddy customization depth** — clothing · items · location · furniture categories
- **Broader Ally types** (beyond friends) + fuller Support Circle
- **Community Insights / Journey reviews** (Bible §8); **Journey Template** foundations
- **Subscription / monetization**
- **Grace Tokens (full system)** — gifting from Allies, per-Journey flexibility policy, regenerating floor + earned top-ups, and Buddy/personalization adaptation from the captured reasons.

---

## V4 — Scale / Ecosystem · *"The platform & the vision"*

Goal: creator/business ecosystem and the differentiated AI/experience layer that approaches the vision.

- **Full Marketplace + Creator economy** — creators publish, monetize, creator profiles (Explore "Top creators")
- **Business Journeys** — brands publish content (Explore "From brands")
- **Rich Step Types system** (Bible §35) — video · audio · quiz · reflection · meditation · PDF · slides · AI-conversation · in-app exercise
- **Interactive Journey Experiences** (§5A.7)
- **Buddy voice & conversations**
- **AI-generated personal roadmaps** · **full dynamic Intervention Engine (JITAI)**
- **Advanced psychological personalization** · **Competition Mode** (§31)
