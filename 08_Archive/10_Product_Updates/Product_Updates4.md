## Approved Decisions

### The Product Exists To Help People Become Who They Choose To Be

After multiple discussions, the vision became significantly more focused.

PushApp is not a habit tracker.

It is not a task manager.

It is not a productivity app.

It is a personal growth platform whose purpose is helping people become who they choose to be.

Current preferred vision statement:

> **Helping people become who they choose to be.**

This sentence should become the foundation for product decisions.

Whenever a feature is discussed, the primary question should be:

"Does this help people become who they choose to be?"

If not, it probably does not belong in PushApp.

---

### Dreams Instead Of Endless Goals

One of the philosophical shifts was separating dreams from execution.

People naturally think about becoming:

- healthier
- happier
- stronger
- wealthier
- more confident

These ambitions should never become endless checklists.

Instead:

Dreams inspire.

Journeys create progress.

This distinction is fundamental to the entire product philosophy.

---

### Progress Over Perfection

PushApp should never communicate perfection.

The objective is sustainable growth.

Examples:

Missing one workout should not invalidate a healthy identity.

Missing one lesson should not erase weeks of learning.

Consistency matters.

Perfection does not.

This philosophy should influence:

- notifications
- Buddy conversations
- Journey rules
- completion logic
- AI coaching

---

### The Product Never Judges

This principle became even stronger during the recent discussions.

PushApp never tells users they failed.

Instead the product observes reality.

Examples:

Instead of:

"You failed."

Prefer:

"It looks like this Journey no longer matches your current routine."

Instead of:

"You broke your streak."

Prefer:

"We've lost momentum.
Would you like to continue, restart or adapt the Journey?"

Language matters.

The product should always protect intrinsic motivation.

---

### Real Life Comes First

The application should never become more important than the user's life.

If reality changes:

PushApp adapts.

Journeys adapt.

Schedules adapt.

Buddy adapts.

The user should never feel trapped by the system they installed to improve their life.

---

### Growth Should Feel Rewarding

The game layer exists to celebrate growth.

Not to replace it.

Players should primarily feel proud because:

they actually learned something

they actually exercised

they actually became more consistent

The game simply amplifies that feeling.

The game must never become more important than reality.

---

### Social Accountability Without Social Pressure

Friends exist to support.

Not to judge.

Not to compete.

Competition may exist later.

Support is the default.

Examples:

Helping friends

Checking in

Sending encouragement

Celebrating completion

Supporting difficult weeks

The emotional tone should always remain positive.

---

### AI Should Feel Invisible

One of the strongest ideas discussed is that users should rarely feel they are "using AI".

Instead they should simply feel that:

Buddy understands them.

Examples:

Buddy learns:

preferred hours

notification style

language

motivation style

Journey preferences

without requiring complicated configuration.

AI is infrastructure.

Buddy is the experience.

---

### PushApp Is Built Around Hope

This became one of the strongest philosophical insights.

Many people already know what they want.

What they lack is:

consistency

support

belief

structure

PushApp exists to restore those.

The product should always communicate hope.

Never shame.

Never guilt.

Never pressure.

---

## Future Vision

### Personalized Growth System

Over time PushApp should learn:

what motivates the user

what discourages them

what Journey structures work best

what notification timing succeeds

what Ally relationships help most

Eventually every user experiences a different version of PushApp.

---

### Growth Marketplace

Long-term vision includes:

coaches

therapists

businesses

course creators

community leaders

creating Journeys that thousands of users can join.

PushApp becomes a platform rather than only an application.

---

### Interactive Growth Experiences

Future PushApp-created Journeys may contain fully interactive experiences.

Examples:

Daily gratitude

Weekly planning

Reflection

Values exercises

NLP exercises

Guided writing

Visualization

These experiences happen inside PushApp instead of simply asking users to report completion.

---

## Open Questions

### Competition

Should competitive mechanics exist?

Current direction:

Support first.

Competition later (if ever).

---

### AI Pricing

Current assumption:

Core PushApp remains valuable without AI.

AI personalization becomes one of the premium subscription benefits.

Still open.

---

### Product Scope

A recurring concern throughout the discussions was product complexity.

The current philosophy is:

Do not reduce the long-term vision.

Instead build the vision gradually.

The repository should therefore clearly distinguish between:

POC

MVP

First Commercial Version

Long-Term Vision

to avoid accidentally shrinking the product vision because of implementation constraints.

---

### Repository Update Notes

These principles should override earlier wording wherever conflicts exist.

Future documentation should consistently reflect:

Growth over productivity.

Support over pressure.

Hope over guilt.

Real-life transformation over virtual progress.

Buddy over AI.

Journeys over endless goals.

Dreams over vague ambitions.
