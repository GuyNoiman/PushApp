# Research

Status: Living Document

## Purpose

Collect evidence that validates or invalidates the assumptions behind PushApp.

---

## High Priority Research

### Behavior Change
- Identity-based behavior change
- Self-efficacy
- Growth mindset
- Long-term habit formation

### Just-In-Time Adaptive Interventions (JITAI)
Research:
- Context-aware interventions
- Optimal intervention timing
- When not to intervene
- Personalization strategies

### Coaching & Accountability
Research:
- Homework adherence
- Accountability between sessions
- Maintaining momentum
- Digital coaching effectiveness

### Social Support
Research:
- Asking for help
- Peer accountability
- Structured support circles
- Emotional vs practical support

### Competition Psychology
Research:
- Healthy competition
- Motivation through challenges
- Cooperative vs competitive systems
- Avoiding toxic leaderboards

### Intervention Engine
Validate:
- Which interventions increase completion?
- Which channels work best?
- Push vs friend vs AI vs silence
- Measuring intervention quality

---

## Competitive Research Framework

For each competitor document:
- Target audience
- Core problem
- Motivation mechanisms
- Personalization
- Social features
- AI capabilities
- Retention strategy
- Pricing
- Strengths
- Weaknesses
- Opportunities for PushApp

---

## Open Research Questions

- Does identity-based messaging outperform goal-based messaging?
- How much does social accountability improve completion?
- Can intervention timing outperform fixed reminders?
- What creates sustainable motivation over months?

## Additional Research Areas (consolidated 2026-07-06)

Merged from the former `04_Product/Roadmap/Research.md`. Each still needs evidence + product implications:

- Attention Economy (screen time, interruption frequency, notification effects on attention)
- Goal Setting (written goals, deadlines, evidence-based frameworks)
- Accountability (individual vs social)
- Habit Formation (how habits form, real timelines, predictors of consistency)
- Identity-Based Change (identity, self-efficacy, growth mindset)
- Digital Interventions (JITAI, context-aware reminders, geofencing)
- Community & Peer Support (peer accountability; support / recovery / learning communities)

Deep, cited evidence for several of these already exists in `Research_Foundation.md`.
