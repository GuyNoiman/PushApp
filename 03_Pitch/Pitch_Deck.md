## 2. The Problem

People rarely fail because they don't know what they want.
They fail because long-term personal change is difficult to sustain.

Most people never intentionally design who they want to become. Even those who do often lose momentum as daily life takes over.

Modern technology is excellent at capturing attention, but remarkably poor at helping people make meaningful progress.

This can be pictured as a whirlpool: a person occasionally lifts their head above the water, decides to change, and starts moving forward — and then the current slowly pulls them back to where they started. PushApp is designed to be the guide that helps people escape that current.

People want to:
- Quit smoking
- Learn new skills
- Improve relationships
- Stay healthy
- Grow professionally

Yet most meaningful personal goals fail because people struggle to remain consistent over time.

## Updates – July 2026

### Refined Mission
**Helping people become who they choose to be.**

PushApp exists to close the gap between intention and action. Most people already know what they want to achieve, but life, distractions, habits and lack of continuous support pull them away from their goals.

### Why Now
- Modern life contains more distractions than ever before.
- People increasingly consume content in short, interactive sessions rather than long courses.
- AI enables a persistent, personalized companion that can support users between coaching sessions, reminders and conversations.
- Users are now comfortable interacting with AI on a daily basis.

### Initial ICP
Young adults who want to build and maintain meaningful habits and personal goals across different areas of life. (Positioning, not a single vertical; go-to-market segments to be defined later.)

### Initial Go-To-Market Hypothesis
Launch through short-form social media (Instagram/TikTok) aimed at self-improvement audiences, with sharing and friend invitations as the primary viral loop.

### Business Model Hypotheses
- Freemium subscription.
- Premium AI capabilities.
- Paid / premium Journeys.
- Business Journeys created by brands.
- Journey creator revenue sharing.
- Optional virtual economy.

### Moat
PushApp's long-term advantage is not only AI but the proprietary understanding of what helps different people successfully complete meaningful personal journeys. Every completed journey improves future interventions.

### Magic Moment
The first moment a user realizes:
> "I didn't think I could stay consistent... but I actually did."
