# Product Bible Draft

> This document contains product hypotheses that have not yet become official product decisions.

# Business Model Hypotheses

## Freemium

Free users will be able to experience the core product.

Premium users may receive:

- Unlimited quests
- AI coaching
- Advanced analytics
- Premium interventions
- Premium customization

Status: Hypothesis

---

## Creator Economy

Creators may publish public quests.

Popular creators may receive revenue sharing based on usage.

Status: Hypothesis

---

## Business Quests

Businesses may create branded quests around their products or services.

Possible monetization:

- Publishing fee
- Revenue share
- Premium placement

Status: Hypothesis

---

## Virtual Economy

Possible future mechanics:

- Coins
- Cosmetics
- Rare avatar items
- Seasonal rewards

Status: Hypothesis

---

## AI Coach

Future premium feature.

Users can discuss goals, create personalized journeys and receive adaptive coaching.

Status: Hypothesis

---

## Competition Mode

Friends can compete on different quests with the same duration.

The objective is motivation, not leaderboard dominance.

Status: Hypothesis

---

## Go-To-Market Hypothesis

Initial acquisition channels:

- Instagram
- TikTok
- Creator partnerships
- Friend invitations

Status: Hypothesis

---

## Beachhead Market

Current hypothesis:

Young adults already engaged in meaningful personal journeys who struggle with consistency.

Status: Under Research
