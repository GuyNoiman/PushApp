# Purpose

This document tracks product ideas, strategic questions and future opportunities that have not yet been validated or approved.

Items remain here until one of the following happens:

- They are approved and moved into an official document.
- They are rejected.
- They become irrelevant.

# Product Discovery

## Helping users discover who they want to become

Explore future experiences that help users identify meaningful personal goals before creating quests.

Potential directions:

- AI-guided reflection conversations
- Guided self-discovery exercises
- Life area assessments

Priority: Future

---

## Growth Library

A library of curated growth journeys that users can browse even if they don't yet know what to start.

Could include:

- Quests
- Courses
- Programs
- Challenges
- Expert content

Priority: Future

---

## Quest Marketplace

Allow experts, coaches and creators to publish high-quality quests.

Open questions:

- Review system
- Monetization
- Quality assurance
- Discovery

Priority: Future

---

## Context-Aware Interventions

Use context to surface the right action at the right time.

Ideas include:

- Geofencing
- Calendar awareness
- App usage
- Time of day
- Routine detection

Priority: Future

---

## Private Support

Allow users to receive accountability without exposing the content of their quest.

Open question:

How much information should allies see?

Priority: Open

---

## Future Quest Queue

Allow users to save future quests they intend to start later.

Potential triggers:

- Manual start
- Scheduled date
- Completion of another quest

Priority: Medium

---

## Premium AI

Future paid capabilities:

- Goal discovery
- Personalized planning
- Breaking goals into milestones
- Personalized motivation
- Reflection conversations

Priority: Future

---

## Research Questions

- What creates long-term engagement without becoming addictive?
- How should success be measured?
- What creates the strongest sense of progress?
- Which interventions genuinely increase consistency?
- Which support mechanisms create value without becoming annoying?
