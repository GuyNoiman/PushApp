# What PushApp Is

PushApp is a personal growth platform that helps people move from aspiration to achievement through structured journeys, adaptive support, progress tracking, and meaningful human connection.

It is not a task manager, reminder app, generic habit tracker, or social network.

The product exists to help people stop living on autopilot and become closer to the person they choose to be.

---

# Core Concepts

- **Dream**: A long-term aspiration ("who I want to become"). Never completed, no rewards; provides direction.
- **Journey**: A finite, achievable personal-growth process (the core object), usually up to ~2 months.
- **Step**: The smallest unit of action inside a Journey. (A Journey may optionally group Steps into sequential Phases.)
- **Buddy**: The user's companion and the emotional face of the product; also the user's public identity across the app.
- **Check-in**: A user report that updates Journey progress.
- **Support Circle**: The group of people supporting a specific Journey.
- **Ally**: A single person inside a Support Circle who accepts a support role.
- **Intervention**: Any action intended to increase the user’s probability of progress.
- **Forecast**: A prediction of likely Journey success based on current behavior.
- **Community Insight**: Advice from users who completed a Journey.
- **Growth Library / Marketplace**: Future source of reusable growth Journeys.

# Current Strategic Hypothesis

PushApp should begin with users who already know they want to change something meaningful and need help staying consistent.

The first beachhead market is still open.

---

# Core Innovation Candidate

PushApp should not merely send reminders.

It should learn when, how, and whether to intervene in order to increase each user’s probability of success.

This is currently described as the Intervention Engine.
